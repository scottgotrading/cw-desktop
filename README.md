# SCOTTGO CW Desktop — ChartsWatcher Desktop Edition

A professional desktop trading platform built around **ChartsWatcher**, by SCOTTGO. It turns the CW web app into a native, multi-monitor scanning workspace, then adds a research assistant, an equity-structure module and a full trading journal on top. One app, one look, built for day traders.

## Scanning workspace
- Multi-monitor launcher opens your CW screens (scanners, toplists, alerts, news) exactly where you want them, every session
- Broker-style themes (DAS, IBKR, Webull, ThinkOrSwim, Lightspeed, Sterling and more) plus a full theme editor, density, corner and font controls
- Custom title bar with market-session badge, opening and closing bells, market clock and live feed health
- Always-on-top pinning per window, saved layout profiles you can switch or pick at startup
- Pop out any tab of the GO-Ai Centre, GO-Fundamentals or the Stock Quote into its own window, place it on any screen and dock it back when done

## GO-Ai research assistant
- Reads the stock you are on: live CW data, SEC filings, trade halts, FINRA short volume, Reg SHO, SSR and insider buys and sells
- Per-ticker Summary with score breakdown, risk pills, dilution and warrants detail, cash runway, squeeze score and an earnings-date warning
- Ranked watchlist (0-100 priority), side-by-side compare and an AI read across the whole list
- Chart image analysis: paste a chart and it reads the trend, levels, setup and risk
- Live alerts feed (halts, filings, Reg SHO, your own CW alerts), in-app news reader and per-ticker notes
- Bring your own AI key (Google Gemini has a free tier; Claude, OpenAI, DeepSeek, Grok and custom endpoints supported). The AI explains real data, it never invents numbers

## GO-Fundamentals
- The equity structure behind a ticker in its own window: structure score and grade, cap table and an 8-factor risk radar
- Dilution and warrants, shelf and ATM capacity, offerings history and estimated cash runway, read from SEC filings
- Short data, ownership with institutional holder trends, and full financial statements (income, balance, cash flow, ratios, segments, peers, earnings)
- Built on free SEC EDGAR and FINRA data; an optional free FMP key adds more

## GO-Journal
- Log trades with raw numbers and it computes the rest: P&L, R multiples, planned vs realised risk-reward, hold times
- Playbooks with rule checklists and adherence scoring, missed-trade tracking and reusable mistake tags
- Twenty report tabs including Journal Score, Profit Trifecta, market-conditions splits (your edge when VIX is above 30), drawdown and account balance
- A trading coach that answers from your real figures, plus a daily review that remembers past days
- Broker import from CSV or Excel with presets for 20 brokers, Live and Sim accounts, linked notes with a knowledge graph, export to JSON, CSV or PDF

## Widgets and monitoring
- Stock Quote quick-view that follows the ticker you click, with score, structure, news and your trade history on it
- Market Clock, GO-Markets heatmaps, a notifications bell and a System Status window for every data source

## What you need
- A ChartsWatcher account (this app hosts the CW web platform; it is not a replacement for it)
- A licence key: request one in-app by email, one key covers two of your own devices
- Optional: your own AI key for GO-Ai, and free FRED and FMP keys for the extras that use them

Keys are stored encrypted on your PC. AI requests go straight from your machine to the provider you pick. No behavioural tracking.

**[Install Guide (PDF)](SCOTTGO-CW-Desktop-Install-Guide.pdf)** — the full install and first-launch walkthrough.

### Windows
1. Download the `.exe` installer from the latest release and run it.
2. If Windows SmartScreen shows a blue warning (normal for a new unsigned app), click **More info**, then **Run anyway**.
3. On first run, agree to the terms and activate your licence. No key yet? Choose **Email my key**, enter your email, then paste the key back in to activate.
4. Want a walkthrough? The [Install Guide PDF](SCOTTGO-CW-Desktop-Install-Guide.pdf) is right here in this repo (and bundled in the release zip).

### macOS
1. Download the `.dmg` for your Mac: `arm64` for Apple Silicon, `x64` for Intel.
2. Read `READ-ME-FIRST-Mac.txt` on the release page: the build is unsigned, so macOS needs one extra approval step on first open.
